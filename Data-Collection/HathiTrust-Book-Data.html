Find a HathiTrust Volume ID¶

You can find a book’s HathiTrust volume ID by following these steps:

  • First, search the HathiTrust catalog. I recommend searching with the Advanced Catalog Search.

  • When you find the item that you’re looking for, click “Limited (Search only)” or “Full View,” depending on the copyright status of the item

  • Then copy and paste the HathiID from the URL of the web page that you’re taken to

    • For example:

      • https://babel.hathitrust.org/cgi/pt?id=mdp.39015029970129 -> mdp.39015029970129

      • https://babel.hathitrust.org/cgi/pt?id=uc2.ark:/13960/t7pn9353t&view=1up&seq=7 -> uc2.ark:/13960/t7pn9353t

    • When in doubt, look in the left sidebar for “Permanent link to this book.” The volume ID will follow 2027

      • https://hdl.handle.net/2027/uc2.ark:/13960/t7pn9353t -> uc2.ark:/13960/t7pn9353t
